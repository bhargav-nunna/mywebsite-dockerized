What I am all about.

I am a Cloud architect and executive consultant with a focus on BigData and Machine learning. I enable business transformations by solving problems and creating cost-effective as well as optimized solutions. In the course of my career, I have lead many organizations through platform and architecture transformations with the cloud-native approach. I also delivered large scale migrations from on-premises data centers to cloud. My expertise is in architecting Data platforms and BigData operations using Analytics, Data Lakes and ETL pipelines. I also have experience in developing applications using Machine Learning(ML) and Natural Language Processing (NLP) to solve day to day problems at various stages of operations in an organization. I am a Subject Matter Expert on AWS BigData stack which includes EMR, Glur, Athena, DynamoDB, Data Pipeline, Kinesis, and ElasticSearch.
